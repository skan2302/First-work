About

Coworkstyle is located near the major train station, Gare de Barcelone and close to the Habib Borguiba street, the main Street of the center of Tunis. It is a collaborative workspace perfect for young entrepreneurs, freelancers and students in need of a relaxing yet professional place to work. Coworkshop provides a space where all are welcome. You do not have to make a deposit; just come in, work and enjoy the space. For us, the difference is our strength!

You can fully enjoy working in a quiet and professional environment, having your own personal space. We offer you a wide range of services so you can focus 100% on your work.
